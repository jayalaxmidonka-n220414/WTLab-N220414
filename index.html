CYBERSECURITY AWARENESS

cybercrime helpline-1930

Cybersecurity awareness is about educating individuals and organizations to recognize online threats (like phishing, malware, scams) and practice safe behaviors (strong passwords, MFA, secure data handling) to prevent cyberattacks, turning people into the first line of defense against breaches and data loss, fostering a security-conscious culture, and protecting sensitive information. It's a continuous process of training and vigilance to counter evolving cyber risks in our digital world.

cybersecurity tips:


1.create strong passwords

2.use multifactor authentication

3.use antivirus software

4.change default passwords

5.encrypt sensitive data
